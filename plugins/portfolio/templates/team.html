Our Team

At Henley, we focus on talent and people. Our trainers consist of highly qualified consultants and members of university faculties. With outstanding research records and strong professional backgrounds they turn theoretical concepts in to real business solutions.

As part of our innovative approach to training, Henley exposes delegates to a variety of different trainers throughout the duration of the course. They are selected for their ability to communicate effectively with delegates of all levels and cultures.
